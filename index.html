What We Do

Strong science and uncompromising quality underlies everything we do at AF Pharma Inc. We are focused on three market segments:

  • Development and manufacturing of specialty prescription generics for the US market
  • Quality and regulatory consulting for pharmaceutical companies in the US
  • Development, licensing and export of dietary supplements and OTC medicines for emerging markets

We are able to work with our various development and manufacturing partners in the North East USA to deliver high quality products for our partners and customers.
